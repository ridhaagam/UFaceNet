- `../program.md`
- `../SOUL.md`
- `../CLAUDE.md`
- `../research/FACEXFORMER_V3_NOTES.md`
- `../research/UFACENET_ARCHITECTURE.md`
- `../research/BENCHMARKS.md`
- `../research/EXPERIMENT_QUEUE.md`
